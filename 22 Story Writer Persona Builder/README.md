# 📖 Story Writer Persona Builder

An interactive **Story Writer Persona Builder** that helps users generate AI-powered storytelling personas based on genre, writing style, target audience, and story goals. The application creates structured creative identities to support writers, educators, storytellers, prompt engineers, and AI-assisted content creators.

## 🎯 How It Works

1. Enter the **Story Genre**
2. Enter the **Writing Style**
3. Enter the **Target Audience**
4. Enter the **Story Goal**
5. Click **Generate Story Persona**
6. Instantly receive:
   - Narrative Identity
   - Story Mechanics
   - Creativity Output
   - AI-Generated Story Concept
