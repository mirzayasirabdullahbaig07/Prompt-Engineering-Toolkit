# 📚 Research Prompt Generator

Research Prompt Generator is a modern web-based application that helps users create structured research prompts for AI tools and academic work. It generates a complete research framework based on a topic, research depth, and preferred output format, making it easier to conduct organized and comprehensive research.

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. Enter a research topic.
2. Select the research depth.
3. Choose the preferred output format.
4. Click **Generate Research Prompt**.
5. The application creates a complete research framework including:
   - Introduction
   - Key Concepts
   - Subtopics
   - Research Questions
   - Case Studies
   - Future Scope
   - Conclusion
