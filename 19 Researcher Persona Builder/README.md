# 🔬 Researcher Persona Builder

An interactive AI-powered web application that generates structured **Researcher Personas** based on a selected research domain, methodology, research level, and objectives. This tool helps educators, researchers, students, and prompt engineers quickly create professional research identities for AI prompting, academic planning, and research simulations.

## 💡 How It Works

1. Enter the research domain.
2. Select the research level.
3. Specify the research methodology.
4. Enter the research objective.
5. Click **Generate Research Persona**.
6. Instantly receive:
   - AI Research Persona
   - Research Identity
   - Expertise Tags
   - Research Focus
   - Methodology Overview
   - Expected Research Output
